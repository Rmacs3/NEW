About FireFly

This four-day music festival in Delaware has been featuring big-name and emerging live acts since 2012. Over 100 bands perform throughout the event across seven different stages.Held at Dover International Speedway, the expansive grounds include restaurants, bars, a market and even campsites.

  • The festival is all ages. Eight (8) years and younger are free in GA. Two (2) years and younger are free in VIP.
  • Attendees 17 years or younger must be accompanied by an adult age 18 years or older.
  • Everyone is subject to search at Festival entrance gates and in the campgrounds.
  • Festival is rain or shine.
